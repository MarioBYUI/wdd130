Believing In God

Does It Makes Sense?

I was baptized when I was 16 years old and I remember I had some troubles with my companions and friends who made mock of my conversion to The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Days Saints because for them doing something more than recognizing your belief in God was too much... Being baptized and committing themselves to participate in religion was too much for them.

Being a Metal Head or a fan of Heavy Metal music did not make sense to my friends my conversion to the Gospel of Jesus Christ because it was something in opposition to what some bands of music were preaching in their lyrics. I could understand that because the idea that they had about religion was about fanatism, "holy wars", making a man or priest rich with my offerings of money, etc. For my friends, it was something so stupid to do to become a member of the church. They thought I was brainwashed...

Nevertheless they did not know the doctrine of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Days Saints. There is sense and there is a logic in believing in God when you know we are his children, that there is a plan of hapinness, and that death is not the end, that we have a purpose here on earth to become more like Christ, that this time is a time of opportunity to be better and to help other people, to form a family and to be sealed with them for this life and all eternity. There are commandments given by God to help us in the way, there are promises we have to make with God for us and for our family. The scriptures help us to know what to do, there is the Holy Ghost or Holy Spirit of God who is here to help us, to guide us, to confort us, to clean us, to purify us, among other things.

Can the reader understand that there is a logic behind what we do? There is sense in what we believe when you understand and know the doctrine inside our beliefs. For us there the member of the church there is hope, there is guidance in the way back to heaven where we belong, there is meaning in our life, we know what to do and we strive to do it the best that we can. There are many ways and ideas and philosophies in this world that confuse us the people, nevertheless, we know what direction to take.
