Welcome To Stop And Think

On this site, you will find articles in English and some of them especially translated into Spanish to reach more people with edifying media. Articles such as "Tolerance vs Truth", or "Sharing the Gospel vs Contention" will be posted here to help anybody who wishes to receive more help. A question will be posted where you can choose what article we should write.

I have seen many people living the Gospel with different capabilities, all of them fighting their fights, but all of them are living and applying the teachings of the Lord Jesus Christ. And because of that, I want to show other people some spiritual thoughts from the doctrine of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Days Saints. The ideas and everything I post on this web page are only my thoughts and ideas and this is not an official site for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Days Saints.
