Overview

Purpose

This Website is to share the light of the Gospel of the Lord Jesus Christ. The idea is to uplift and edify other people with articles, talks, videos, personal stories, among other positive things. The world is full with bad things and especially it is full with noise and propaganda. This website is to help to contrarrest that giving the people an uplifting moment.

Audience

This website is directed to any person who is looking for inspiration and conection with the heavens. It does not matter their belief, everyone is invited.

Home page

Welcome to this site! In this site you will find articles especially translated to Spanish that you might be able to understand them. Articles suchs as "Tolerance vs Truth", or "Sharing the Gospel vs Contention" will be posted here to help anybody who whishes to receive more help. A question will be posted where you can choose what article we should write.

I have seen many people living the Gospel with different capabilities, all of them fighting their own fights, but all of them are living and applying the teachings of the Lord Jesus Christ. And because of that I want to show other people some spiritual thoughts from the doctrine of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Days Saints. The ideas and everything I post in this web page are only my thoughts and ideas and this is not a official site for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Days Saints.

Articles

How should I act when others are breaking the commandments? How can I be tolerant and at the same time defend the truth? Ultimately I have found myself not being sure about when I am being tolerant and when I am not defending the truth as I should. I have been studying some talks that are in English which I will translate to the Spanish speaking people can read them. We have counsel from the living Prophets and many of their talks are based on questions asked from other people.

I will also share about how to deal with difficult question from other people, and I will share some ideas to focus on when sharing the gospel with other people.

I will study deeply the general conference talk and, wiht the time, I will make a podcast with a friend who is interested in study the general conference talk in a deep way.

I love psychology and philoophy and I will share some truth that we can find there that can help us to be better and to understand ourselves a little bit more. There are many troubles we carry with us that are from our child's years and hopefuly with what I will share people can find healing from them. I know the Lord heals us in different things and the more we look for healing the more we will recieve.

Finally there is wisdom in understanding the wordly doctrines and philosophies and their hollow foundations and bad consequences. There is logic in following Jesus Christ and I will strive to show to you this path of light and truth in comparison to the wordly philosophies and ideas. Visitors to this site will be able to analyse this two sides and will be able to choose in a more informed manner.

Blog

Living The Gospel With Anxiety Disorders

My life has been filled with axiety and fear to many things that are only in my mind. How would someone with this challenges can live the Gospel? Here in this blog entry I would tell how my anxious life has been blessed with the Gospel and how the teachings of the Lord Jesus Christ has been helping me to deal with my fears. I will write some blog entry about Mindfulness and information about the latest studies and researches related to mental health and emotional inteligence.

I will share some reviews of some books that have been helping me to deal with many things from my character that I consider important that other may know about these books, that they might improve as well and find help to their challenges. We can learn from many different things such as books, articles, art, music, speeches, etc. I will try to cover a little bit of each one of them.
