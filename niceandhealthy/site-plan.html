Overview

Purpose

Have you ever found yourself tired of eating fast food and wanting to eat something healthy and good for your body and not finding anything fresh and healthy? What would you say if I tell you that I built a web page where you can choose for example 15 ounces of tomato, 10 of lettuce, 17 of rice, and 10 of chicken meat and be sent to you? Our page enables the customer to mix his own healthy food out of more than 20 products that we sell by ounces. There are no pre-made salads, the customer chooses the quantity of whatever he or she wants.

Audience

This web site is intended for everyone who wants to eat “Nice and Healthy” as our name evokes, but our main intention is directed to the people who is working and who is looking for a place to eat something good and something healthy.

Content

Home page

Nice and Healthy is the answer to everyone who has always wanted to eat healthier but who does not have the time to use in the preparation of healthy food and who does not know where to buy every vegetable that there is to satisfy their needs. Nice and Healthy will help those people by showing them all the menu on our site and then, they will choose whatever they want for lunch.
People will come to our site because they will be able to choose exactly what and how much they want to eat. There is no pre-made food, there are only vegetables and different kinds of rice where they can choose what and the quantity they want. There is no other site here in our city where customers can do that. There are some sites of healthy food offering their services but all of them are made of pre-made menus and not one site offers what we offer. That is our difference, that it is our brand.
On our site, the customer will be able to see the different types of vegetables, such as carrots, lettuce, tomato, potatoes, onion, bell pepper, broccoli, cabbage, corn, cauliflower, green bean, pee, white and integral rice, boiled eggs, and some seeds that are able to be chosen by our customers. The site will provide the customers the images of our veggies and the prices and then the customers will only need to choose among them the quantity and then make the order. The site will give them the total price of their order.
So, our most important part as a web page will be to show what we have to the customers, and we will make them able to make an order among what we have.

Blog

Our site will also have a Blog site where our customers will be able to read about some important issues related to healthy living and healthy food, such as, what aliments I should avoid if I want to diminish the sugar in my blood, or, what are the best characteristics of the lettuce, for example, or, what is the right amount of rice for a person per day. This will be done with the intent to help the customers know more about issues related to their personal well-being.

Images for the Blog

Meet Our Suppliers

Lastly, our site will have a page where we would name who are the suppliers of our vegetables and more. The idea is to show the people where our “ingredients” are coming from. We will show the name of the people who provide us and also a picture and the location where they are from.
